Personal Plan

  1. Bar all ground-level doors (my house doors have slots to insert a metal bar to keep people out)
  2. Board up all windows (basement, main floor and upstairs)
  3. ^ Board up basement door, as well (no bar slot, so easy target)
  4. Hold out with food and drinks stocked at the time
  5. Supplies would diminish in 2 or 3 days, so definitely scavenge (with a group)
  6. Hold out in house for however long the undead and other people let us
  7. If forced out of house, use only silent weapons and use stealth to remain undetected
  8. Keep on the go and find a new home hidden from general view, some place discrete
  9. Repeat steps when or if necessary

Possible Zombie Scenarios

  1. The virus kills living people and brings them back, *possibly* allowing them to think somewhat normally
  2. The virus raises the already-dead from their graves, with people freaking out and trying to escape
  3. Depending on the transmition option(s) of the virus, survival may or may not be possible
